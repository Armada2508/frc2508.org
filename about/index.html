Mission

The mission of Armada Robotics is to offer students the opportunity to gain leadership skills, hands-on learning experience, and acquire real-world techniques within the STEM field all while having fun. We also do not cost members a fee to join, we only require motivation and a willingness to learn, empowering any student with any background to develop a passion for STEM to build the next generation of engineers.

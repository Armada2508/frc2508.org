Why donate to Stillwater Armada Robotics?

Support future leaders.

Although this robotics team has several amazing coaches and mentors, the majority of Armada's functions are student-led. Many of our members will move on to universities and careers that will utilize the leadership skills learned right here in Armada Robotics.

Foster innovation and creativity.

Here at Armada, we use knowledge and experimentation in woodworking, metalworking, software development, networking, electronics, wiring, and telecommunications, to achieve success. Help keep the flame of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics alive by contributing to our robotics team's efforts.

Encourage collaboration.

Armada upholds a strong sense of cooporation between different small groups of people that focus on mechanical, electrical, business, and programming. Armada would be unsuccessful without collaboration, just as many of the world's greatest organizations.
